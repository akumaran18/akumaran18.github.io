About





Donut Delights was founded in 1971 and has been operating on Main Street for 47 years. We are a family owned company and take great pride in serving up fresh, homemade recipes to the residents of Champaign - Urbana. All of our preparations- including icing and batter- are made from scratch daily by our first shift. We use local ingredients. As members of Buy Fresh Buy Local Central Illinois and the Illinois Stewardship Alliance, we can guarantee that each of our products- even our coffee- supports Illinois businesses and farmers, making them naturally nutritious and deicious.
